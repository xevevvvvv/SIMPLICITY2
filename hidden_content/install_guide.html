Installation Instructions

1 Locate the downloaded file

After downloading the installation file, locate it at the top right corner of your browser or in the download folder of your computer.

2 Start the installation

Double click on the downloaded file to start the installation.

3 Click "More info"

A Microsoft Defender SmartScreen warning will appear. Click on "More info".

⚠️ Important: Do not click "More info" in the image below. The image is just a reference. You need to click "More info" in the actual Windows dialog that appears on your computer.

4 Click "Run anyway"

The "Run anyway" option will appear; click it to proceed and complete the installation.
